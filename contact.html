How long does a website take to build?

The time it takes to build a website depends on various factors such as complexity, design, features, and the development process. Here’s a general breakdown:
‍
‍Simple website (e.g., personal blogs or portfolio sites): 1-3 weeks.
‍
‍Small business website (with custom design, 5-10 pages): 4-6 weeks.

E-commerce or more complex site (with multiple features, integrations):
8-12 weeks or more.The timeline also depends on client feedback, revisions, and any specific customization required..
