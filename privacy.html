2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for:
‍
● Providing and improving our services.

● Responding to inquiries or requests.

● Running marketing campaigns, including targeted ads.

● Measuring performance of ads and website interactions.

● Ensuring security, fraud prevention, and compliance.

Our website uses cookies and tracking technologies (such as Meta Pixel, Google Analytics, and similar tools) to:
